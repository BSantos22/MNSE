Mikel Arteta told he can replace Pep Guardiola at Manchester City

Manchester City want Mikel Arteta to take over from Pep Guardiola and are ready to hand the Spaniard a new contract, according to reports.

The former Arsenal captain did have his heart set on becoming Arsene Wenger’s successor and was gutted to learn the Gunners had decided to appoint Unai Emery.

Arteta’s lack of experience in a managerial role seems to have been the deciding factor in Arsenal’s final decision.

Arsenal have opted for experience in Unai Emery who has previously been in charge at Paris Saint-Germain, Sevilla, Spartak Moscow and Valencia.
