Di Maria in talks with Napoli

The 30-year-old Argentina international's is due to expire next summer, meaning he would be available for an affordable price.

Pep Guardiola considers making move for Matthijs de Ligt

The Ajax defender could replace an aging Vincent Kompany at Manchester City.

De Ligt fits the profile of a young, technically astute defender who could slot into City’s playing style.

The 18-year-old defender has made 34 appearances in all competitions this season, and could provide consistency unlike Kompany, who is an ongoing injury concern to Guardiola. The captain is also entering the final year of his contract and, while he may be considered for an extension, there are no guarantees.
